New Patient Information

Welcome to First Line Dental! We’re changing the way people perceive dentistry by offering personalized, one-on-one care that always goes the extra mile. If you’re brand-new to our dental office, we invite you to read the following information on this page so that you know what to expect from your initial appointment and the financial options that are available to you. Any questions or concerns? Please don’t hesitate to contact us directly in Manchester, CT.

Your First Visit

From the moment you walk through our front door, our team members are fully focused on making sure your experience is comfortable and rewarding. You’ll be greeted by name, followed by a comprehensive examination and a one-on-one discussion regarding your dental needs, goals, and concerns. A treatment plan will be designed for you based on our findings that’s comprehensively custom. We’ll be happy to help you understand your dental insurance coverage and available payment options. Our goal is to leave you feeling more confident than ever about your smile.

Patient Registration Form

Dental Technology

First Line Dental is located in a brand-new, fully digital office! Patients will be able to experience the benefits of several modern dental technologies firsthand here, including all-digital X-rays with reduced radiation, educational intraoral cameras, and more. This state-of-the-art approach results in greater diagnostic precision, added efficiency, and a more comfortable experience overall.

New Patient Offers

Being able to save money on a much-needed service can make any day a little brighter. That’s why First Line Dental is happy to feature multiple new patient offers that patients can take advantage of! Just be sure to mention them to our team members when scheduling an appointment. These specific deals may change over time.

Explore Our New Patient Offers

Dental Insurance

Our team goes above and beyond to ensure that all patients have access to quality dental care at a reasonable price. That’s why First Line Dental accepts most major dental insurance plans. Our knowledgeable team members are more than capable of filing claims on the patient’s behalf, simplifying the reimbursement process and maximizing benefits to their fullest extent. We’ll also be sure to review all available coverage thoroughly with you before any treatment begins so that your financial obligations are completely clear from the get-go.

Essential Dental Plan

Do you not currently have dental insurance? Our dental office accepts Essential Dental Plan, which for a flat yearly fee, gives patients and families free exams and x-rays, discounted rates on restorative treatment, orthodontics, and significant savings on all treatment. It can help keep your dental care on a budget. (The Essential Dental Plan is not insurance.)

Learn About Essential Dental

Dental Financing

If your family currently isn’t covered by a dental insurance plan or would like to schedule extensive treatment, low-interest payment plans are available through CareCredit. This trusted third-party company offers several lengths of financing, which allows patients to choose the repayment schedule that best fits their monthly budget. To see if you qualify, ask us about completing an application.
